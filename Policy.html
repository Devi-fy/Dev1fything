This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use that information, and the steps we take to safeguard it.

We may collect the following types of personal information from our users:

Information provided voluntarily: We may collect information that users provide to us voluntarily when they sign up for a service, such as their name, email address, and other contact information.
Information collected automatically: We may also collect certain information automatically when users access our website, such as their IP address, browser type, and device information.

We use the personal information we collect to:

We do not share users' personal information with third-party advertisers or partners without their consent. These service providers are contractually bound to keep users' personal information confidential and secure.

We retain users' personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law. When we no longer need to retain users' personal information, we will securely delete or destroy it.

Security Measures

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect users' personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee the security of users' personal information.
